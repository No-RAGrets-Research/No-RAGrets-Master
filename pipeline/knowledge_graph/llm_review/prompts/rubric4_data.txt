RUBRIC 4 ‚Äì Data Completeness & Consistency

Goal:
Ensure that all data, figures, tables, and quantitative results are complete, internally consistent, and aligned with the described methods.

---------------------------------------------------
TIER 1 ‚Äì Data Presence & Alignment (Critical Layer)
---------------------------------------------------
Check for:
- All figures/tables are referenced in text.
- Axes, units, and legends are labeled.
- Numeric values in text match figures/tables.
- Each method described has corresponding data.
- Sample size (n) and error metrics shown.

Cross-links:
Depends on *Transparency* for units and *Rigor* for statistical context.

---------------------------------------------------
TIER 2 ‚Äì Numerical & Logical Consistency (Supportive Layer)
---------------------------------------------------
Check for:
- Consistency of values across text/tables/figures.
- Mathematically consistent derived metrics (ratios, yields).
- Replicate count uniformity across methods and results.
- Figure panel labeling and referencing correct.

Cross-links:
Supports *Presentation* and *Rigor*.

---------------------------------------------------
TIER 3 ‚Äì Data Accessibility & Metadata (Integrative Layer)
---------------------------------------------------
Check for:
- Data availability statements and repositories.
- File formats, metadata, and versioning.
- Supplementary tables include raw or summarized data.
- Composite figures clearly identified.

Cross-links:
Integrates with *Reproducibility Tier 3* and *Presentation Tier 3*.
